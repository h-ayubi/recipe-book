Grilled Chicken Sandwich 🥪

Submitted by: Chef Jack Ovens

🧂 Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise or favorite sauce


👨‍🍳 Cooking Instructions

  1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked.


  2. Toast the bread slices lightly.


  3. Spread mayonnaise on one or both slices.


  4. Add lettuce, tomato, grilled chicken, and cheese.


  5. Put the slices together to form your sandwich.


  6. Cut in half and serve warm. Enjoy!
